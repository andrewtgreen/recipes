Ground Turkey Taco Meat Recipe

Ingredients for ground turkey taco meat

  • Ground turkey
  • Ground cumin
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Onion and Garlic Powder
  • Oregano, Basil, Thyme, Rosemary
  • Brown Sugar
  • Lime juice
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat pan on medium heat with olive oil
  2. Add turkey, break up, and begin to brown
  3. Add all the seasonings, add lime juice
  4. Cook until brown and oil is absorbed
