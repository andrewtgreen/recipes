Banana Oatmeal Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients for three servings of pancakes

  • 2 medium ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups plain oats
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until completely smooth
  2. Heat up pan and add olive oil
  3. For each pancake, cook 1/3 cup of batter on medium heat for 2-4 minutes, flip and cook
  4. Adjust heat to medium low if too hot
