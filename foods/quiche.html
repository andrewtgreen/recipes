Quiche Recipe

Ingredients for one quiche

  • Pie crust
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli
  • 1 bunch sliced honey smoked turkey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Put pie crust in pie dish and poke holes the bottom
  3. Pre-bake pie crust for 6 minutes then take out and put on stovetop
  4. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs, add milk and beat a few strokes, stir in broccoli and shredded turkey
  5. Pour contents into pie dish with crust, put more cheese on top
  6. Bake for 60-70 minutes
