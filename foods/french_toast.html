French Toast Recipe

Ingredients for two pieces of french toast

  • 2 pieces italian white bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Heat up pan on medium heat and butter the surface
  2. Whisk the egg in a shallow, wide tray with the cinnamon and vanilla, stir milk into the mix
  3. Dip one side of each slice of bread in mix and soak for 15 seconds, flip and soak
  4. Place bread on pan and cook each side until golden brown
